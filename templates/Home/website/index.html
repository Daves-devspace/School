School Facilities

Our school provides top-notch facilities designed to ensure a safe, engaging, and supportive environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and physically.

School Bus

Our school bus service ensures safe and timely transportation for all students, covering major routes with experienced drivers and attendants.

Playground

We provide a well-equipped playground to encourage physical activities, teamwork, and a healthy lifestyle among students.

Healthy Canteen

Our canteen offers nutritious and balanced meals, promoting healthy eating habits and catering to dietary preferences.

Positive Learning

We create a positive learning environment where students are inspired to grow academically and emotionally with the support of qualified teachers.

Learn More About Our Work And Our Cultural Activities

At Merryland, we strive to provide a holistic educational experience that blends academics with rich cultural activities. Our programs are designed to nurture creativity, teamwork, and personal growth, ensuring students are well-rounded and prepared for the future.

Through various cultural events and interactive sessions, we encourage students to explore diverse traditions, build connections, and develop a sense of community. Join us in fostering a vibrant and inclusive environment where every child thrives.
